The customer's 7.5% buys

  • A tradesperson who shows up preparedVetted, and does it right first time
  • Money kept safeEscrow releases only when it's done
  • A clear priceThe price is known up front and the customer's payment is protected
  • Paperwork handledWork contract and documents sorted — nothing to deal with
  • Recourse if it goes wrongSkildo holds the money and helps resolve any dispute

The tradesperson's 7.5% buys

  • Work without hunting for itA stream of nearby jobs — no marketing, no cold calls
  • A well-described jobFull brief up front — no survey trip
  • Sure, instant payoutMoney already in escrow; paid on approval, no chasing
  • Admin handledInvoice, tax reporting (EET 2.0) and the contract — done for them
  • A basic CRMCustomer database, calendar and reminders
